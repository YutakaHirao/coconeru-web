About

About CoconeRu.

CoconeRu. is a local wellness brand in central Yokosuka, close to the Naval Base and a 4-minute walk from Yokosuka-chuo Station on the Keikyu Line. We run two quiet, unhurried spaces: CoconeRu. Head Spa, a dry head spa built around rest and better sleep, and CoconeRu. Move, a personal training and pilates studio focused on posture and everyday movement. Both are open daily from 10:00 to 20:00 by reservation. Our aim is simple: a calm, well-kept place where you can slow down and feel looked after, without noise or hard selling.

Head Spa

Dry Head Spa & Head Massage

The dry head spa is a relaxing head and scalp massage given without water, shampoo, or oil, so you stay dressed and can return to your day easily afterward.

In a low-lit room we ease tension through the head, around the eyes, and across the neck and shoulders, then extend the same calm to the hands, face, and back. It is relaxation rather than medical or clinical treatment, and it suits anyone carrying screen fatigue, tired eyes, or a stiff neck at the end of a long day. Many guests come to unwind before sleep. Head Spa is on the 3rd floor of First Building, 3-4 Wakamatsu-cho, Yokosuka.

Move

Personal Training & Pilates

CoconeRu. Move is a one-to-one personal training studio in a bright, friendly space, pairing machine (reformer) and mat pilates with hands-on conditioning to help you move and sit more comfortably.

Sessions are private and built around you, whether your goal is better posture, easing a stiff neck or shoulders, building a steady exercise habit, or working gradually toward a fitter, stronger body. Beginners and people returning to exercise after a long break are very welcome, and both men and women train here. There is no pressure to push hard, only to keep going at a pace that lasts. Move is on the 2nd floor of Yashima Building, 2-11 Otaki-cho, Yokosuka.

Access

Access from Yokosuka Naval Base

Both studios sit in central Yokosuka, close to the Naval Base and easy to reach on foot or by a short ride on the Keikyu Line. Yokosuka-chuo Station is the nearest station, and each studio is about a 4-minute walk from there.

Hours & Booking

Hours & Booking

We are open every day from 10:00 to 20:00 and work by reservation, though we will do our best to fit in walk-ins when the schedule allows. The easiest way to book is by phone at 090-7186-4738 or through online booking; a first-visit trial is available at Move.
